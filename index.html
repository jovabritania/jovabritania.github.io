Honey Face Mask

This face mask is used for soothing and calming skin.To make it you need 1 tsp honey, 1 tsp coconut oil, 1/4 ripe avocado, and 2 drops of lavender oil. Like one big glow in a bowl, this delicious-sounding face mask delivers deep hydration from the coconut oil, gives your skin a hit of antioxidants with the honey and soothes redness with the lavender oil. Mix it up and paint it on with a face mask brush for the ultimate at-home spa experience.
